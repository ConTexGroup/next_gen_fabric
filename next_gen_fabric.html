Conbello Textile: Next-Gen Performance Fabric

Traditional fabrics like polyamide and polyester often cause discomfort and potential health problems. They contain high elastane, which leads to close direct contact with the skin, trapping moisture and keeping the skin and fabric wet. This results in excessive sweating, allergic reactions, and an increase in body temperature. Synthetic fabrics like polyamide, which cannot absorb and evaporate moisture, worsen this problem.

Conbello Textile's R&D department developed a new generation air-layer fabric technology to address these deficiencies. This innovative approach includes an air gap, similar to a film strip, between the fabric and the skin. This air layer minimizes fabric-skin contact and friction, significantly reducing sweating and the risk of allergic reactions.

The ultra-soft inner and outer structure provides exceptional comfort in various environments (hot to cold). Unique features include exceptional durability, softness, and 55% spandex content.

Offers a significantly softer feel than traditional polyamide.

Specifically designed for cooler or colder outdoor conditions.

Features a laminated structure that creates an air layer inside the main fabric. This air-lock layer provides an additional barrier against the elements.

Ideal for activities in cold environments, keeping the wearer warmer and drier.
